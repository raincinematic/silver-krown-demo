The Vision

The Crown Was
Always Yours.

Silver Krown Productions exists because the most powerful stories come from the people Hollywood forgot to cast. Women over 40. Women who have lived. Women whose complexity, wisdom, and fire don't fit neatly into a supporting role.

Founded by actress and pro-aging advocate Rolonda Rochelle, Silver Krown is an Atlanta-based production company developing original content that celebrates authentic, complex, and underrepresented voices in film and television.

“We don't wait to be discovered. We build the room — and we own the table.”

We are actively developing, producing, and casting. We are looking for collaborators who believe the best chapters haven't been written yet.

The Founder

Rolonda
Rochelle.

Actress. Producer. Pro-Aging Advocate. Rolonda Rochelle is a Texas native who made Atlanta her home in 1993, drawn by its thriving film and television community. After 40, she made the decision to defy the odds — and she hasn't stopped since.

With 14+ screen credits spanning film, television, and digital series, a community of over 1 million followers, and Silver Krown Productions as her platform, Rolonda is not just an actress — she is a force creating the stories she always deserved to tell.
